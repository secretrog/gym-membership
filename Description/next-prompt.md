A private Photo & Media Gallery for members to track their progress and explore the gym.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Dark, high-energy, premium
- Background: Deep Charcoal (#121212)
- Surface: Fitness Slate (#1E1E1E) for cards and modals
- Primary Accent: Neon Citrus (#D4FF00) for active states and primary actions
- Text Primary: Crisp White (#FFFFFF)
- Text Secondary: Muted Ash (#A0A0A0)
- Buttons: Sharp, squared-off edges (rounded-none or rounded-sm)
- Typography: Bold headers, clean readable body text (Inter).

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** Navigation with logo and user profile.
2. **Tabs/Toggle:** A stylish toggle switch to flip between "My Progress" and "Virtual Tour".
3. **"My Progress" View (Default):**
   - A "Before/After" side-by-side photo comparison component.
   - A masonry grid of uploaded progress photos with dates.
   - An "Upload New Photo" button (styled as primary Neon Citrus).
4. **"Virtual Tour" View:**
   - A "Lightbox" style high-res photo gallery showcasing gym equipment and facilities. Show large thumbnails that look like they can be clicked to expand.
